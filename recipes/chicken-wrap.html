Chicken wrap

Description

This chicken wrap is the eighth wonder of the world. Carbs, fats, protein; the trifecta of nourishment. My teriyaki chicken wrap is guarenteed to have you questioning your life choices, wondering where you went wrong and why you didn't find this page sooner.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast(any cut will do)
  • Tortilla wrap
  • Bell pepper the color of the rainbow
  • Sharp cheddar cheese
  • Diced tomato

Steps

  1. Slice a bell pepper into strips
  2. Dice a tomato
  3. Simmer the teriyaki sauce
  4. Grill chicken on the stovetop in an oil of your choosing
  5. Pour sauce on top of chicken and turn off the heat
  6. Place all the ingredients onto tortilla wrap and, well wrap it
  7. Stick entire wrap into a pan to give it a nice toast
  8. Profit, you're welcome
